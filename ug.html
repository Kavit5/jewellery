A Bachelor of Arts, commonly known as a BA degree, is an undergraduate academic program that falls under the umbrella of the arts and humanities. It's typically a three-year course that provides a broad and well-rounded education in subjects such as literature, history, philosophy, languages, and social sciences.It gives students a broad base in the humanities, social sciences, and other liberal arts. It gives graduates critical thinking, communication, research, and writing skills.
